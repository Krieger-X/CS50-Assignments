The Battlefield series is a popular first-person shooter video game franchise developed by EA DICE and published by Electronic Arts. The series began in 2002 with the release of Battlefield 1942, and has since grown to include multiple titles set in different eras and locations, such as World War II, the modern era, and the future. The games are known for their large-scale multiplayer battles, featuring a wide variety of vehicles and weapons, as well as destructible environments. The series has been praised for its realistic graphics, intense gameplay, and strategic elements. It has a dedicated fan base and have been critically acclaimed for its immersive gameplay, attention to detail and its multiplayer capabilities.
