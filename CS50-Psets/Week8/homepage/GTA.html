The Grand Theft Auto (GTA) series is a critically acclaimed and commercially successful action-adventure video game franchise developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. The series is set in fictional cities modeled after real-world locations, and the player takes on the role of a criminal who rises through the ranks of organized crime through a series of missions. The series has been praised for its open-world gameplay, satirical take on American culture, and engaging storylines. Since the first game's release in 1997, the series has grown to include multiple titles, each with their own unique setting, characters, and gameplay mechanics. The series has become one of the most popular and influential video game franchises of all time, and continues to be a favorite among players worldwide.
