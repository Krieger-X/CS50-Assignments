Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO) is a first-person shooter game developed by Valve Corporation and Hidden Path Entertainment. It was released in 2012 as the fourth game in the Counter-Strike series. The game is played in teams of terrorists and counter-terrorists, with the objective of either planting a bomb or defusing it, rescuing hostages or keeping them held, or eliminating all members of the opposing team. The game has a large competitive scene, with many professional players and organized leagues and tournaments. It also has a large following of casual players who enjoy playing the game for fun.
